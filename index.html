Peanut Butter and Jelly

Description: Peanut butter and jelly is a really good sandwich that is very easy to make!

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Servings: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Peanut
Butter		 3 tbs
Jelly 3 tbs
Bread 2 slices

Directions

  1. Spread the peanut butter on one piece of bread.
  2. Spread the jelly on the other side.
  3. Put the two pieces of bread together to form a sandwich.

Chocolate Mug Cake

Description: This chocolaty fudgy treat is truly decadent and great for nights you need a yummy dessert that is ready in less than 10 minutes!

  • Time: 7 minutes
  • Servings: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1/4 cup
Sugar 1/4 cup
cocoa powder 2 tbs
milk 3 tbs
baking soda 1/8 tsp

Directions

  1. Mix flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, in a large microwave-safe mug; stir in milk
  2. Cook in microwave until cake is done in the middle, about 1 minute 45 seconds.
